Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is sunset?  The time of day when the Sun disappears, descending below the Earth's horizon.
  • What is sunrise?  The time of day when the Sun appears, ascending above the Earth's horizon.
  • How is local time calculated?  Local time is calculated using Google's Time Zone API based on the geographic coordinates of the input location.
  • What locations can I search for?  You can search for any city and country globally.
  • Why doesn't my location show up?  Make sure to enter both the city and country for best results.
